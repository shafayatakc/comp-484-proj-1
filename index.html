The Uncharted Collection

Series Summary

The Uncharted series follows the adventures of Nathan Drake, a treasure hunter who travels across the world to uncover various historical mysteries. The series is known for its cinematic storytelling, engaging gameplay, and memorable characters. Each game explores different locations and myths, making it a beloved franchise among gamers.

Main Characters

  • Nathan Drake: The protagonist, a charismatic and skilled treasure hunter.
  • Elena Fisher: A journalist and Nathan's love interest.
  • Victor Sullivan: Nathan's mentor and close friend.
  • Chloe Frazer: A fellow treasure hunter with a complicated relationship with Nathan.
